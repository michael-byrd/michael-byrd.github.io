What is a Cauchy Sequence?

A Cauchy sequence is a sequence, \( \{x_n\}_{n=1}^{\infty} \) with the property that all but finitely many terms are arbitraily close to each other.

Formally, for every \(\epsilon > 0,\) there exists an \( N \in \mathbb{N} \) such that \( \left| x_n - x_m \right| < \epsilon \) for all \(m,n \geq N\).
